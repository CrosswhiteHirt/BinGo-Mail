BinGo Mail

Sort colorful envelopes into limited mail bins, complete stamp lines, and chase perfect deliveries in a calm offline puzzle.

Mailroom Puzzles

Every level gives you a compact wall of bins and a queue of envelopes. The challenge is deciding where each letter belongs before space runs out.

Stamp Lines

Complete stamp-card goals to deliver a line. Perfect runs ask for clean planning, no conflicts, and one extra level goal.

Offline Play

Progress is saved locally. You can select levels, replay for better stars, and play without an account.
