Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Overview

BinGo Mail is a single-player puzzle game. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account.

Advertising and Tracking Permission

The app may show rewarded ads through third-party advertising SDKs, including Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, and InMobi. On iOS versions that require App Tracking Transparency permission, the app asks for permission before accessing the advertising identifier. If permission is not granted, ads may still be shown using limited or contextual signals.

Identifiers and Security

When tracking permission is granted, the app may use the device advertising identifier to deliver and measure ads. The app also uses the device vendor identifier for security and anti-abuse functions through HSSecurity. Advertising configuration may be requested from a remote configuration service using the app bundle identifier.

Data We Do Not Collect Directly

We do not ask you to create an account, enter your name, upload contacts, or provide precise location inside the game. Local level progress remains on your device unless system backup features copy app data.

Third-Party Services

Third-party SDKs may process device information, identifiers, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals according to their own policies. Their behavior can depend on your iOS privacy settings and whether you grant tracking permission.

Children's Privacy

BinGo Mail is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service, contact us and we will review the issue.
